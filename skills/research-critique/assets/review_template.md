- [ ] Data + code deposited with DOIs/accessions; statements present; links live; no "available upon request."

- [ ] Cited references exist and say what I claim.
- [ ] Mandatory venue furniture present; word/figure budget met.
- [ ] Major vs Minor correctly categorized (Minor not padded into Major).
