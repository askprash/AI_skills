## Confidential comments to the editor

*Only the editor sees this section. Use for:*
- *Integrity concerns (suspected image manipulation, data fabrication, plagiarism). Be specific; don't accuse without evidence.*
- *Reviewer's qualifications and the parts of the paper you could / couldn't evaluate.*
- *Calibration notes ("I lean toward reject but could be swayed if the authors address point 2").*
- *Suspected COI you became aware of during review.*

*Do NOT include:*
- *Comments that should be in the author-facing review.*
- *Personal opinions about the authors.*
- *Accusations without specific evidence.*

## Final self-check before submitting

*Tick off before delivering — these catch the most common mistakes.*

- [ ] Recommendation matches the comments (not "minor revisions" with critical issues unresolved).
- [ ] Every comment is located (section, page, line, figure, equation).
- [ ] Cited references (in the review) actually exist and say what I claim.
- [ ] Tone is constructive throughout; re-read as a whole.
- [ ] No identifying information about myself if review is double-blind.
- [ ] Major and Minor comments are correctly categorized (Minor not padded into Major).
- [ ] Strengths section is genuine, not perfunctory.
- [ ] Length is proportionate to the manuscript.
- [ ] Confidential editor comments separated from author-facing review.
- [ ] No accusations of misconduct in the author-facing review (those go to the editor).
